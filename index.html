Indian Railways

Indian railway is the lifeline loved by millions of people across the country. It plays a huge role in nation-building, whether economic or social.

Mountain Railway

The Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, with the nickname "the Toy Train," is a 610 mm (2.00 ft) narrow-gauge railway that links the 88 km (55 mi) between Siliguri and Darjeeling.

Chenab Railway Bridge

India is now home to the world's tallest railway bridge. Some 29 meters (over 95 feet) taller than the Eiffel Tower, the Chenab Bridge sits 359 meters (around 1,180 feet) above the Chenab River in India's contentious Jammu and Kashmir region.
